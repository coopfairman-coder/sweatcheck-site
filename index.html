The Origin Story

Built by gym rats.
For gym rats.

Founder

Cooper Fairman

Finance · University of Tennessee

I've been in the gym consistently since I was 15. What I kept noticing: the gym is the one place most people want to be consistent, and fail at consistently. Not because they don't care. Because there's no social pressure. Nobody sees when you skip. Nobody sees when you show up 47 straight days.

"If your fitness journey was public, how much harder would you push?"

That question became SweatCheck. The first social platform designed for the gym from day one.

Co-Founder

Kyle Keener

Kinesiology · University of Tennessee

Former competitive athlete. The guy who looked at the fitness app landscape and said the same thing Cooper said: none of these are built for us.

Kyle brings the athlete's perspective to every product decision. When SweatCheck feels like it actually understands how a real training session works. That's him..
