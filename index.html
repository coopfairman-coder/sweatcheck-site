The Concept.

SweatCheck is built around three simple ideas. Show up. Post. Repeat.

01
Lock In

Arrive at the gym and Lock In. No more mindless scrolling — you're here to work.

02
Daily Sweat Post

Make your Daily Sweat Post at any point during your workout. One post, one habit.

03
Build Your Streak

Post daily to grow your Sweat Streak. It won't break for 2 days — rest days are built in.
